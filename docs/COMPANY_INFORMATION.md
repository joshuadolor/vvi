# Victor Vanguard International (VVI)

## Company Overview

Victor Vanguard International (VVI) is the international sourcing, supplier verification, and quality assurance company of the Dolor Group.

Built upon the decades of procurement and retail experience of DVS Dolor Marketing, VVI was established to help businesses source products from China with confidence by providing independent factory verification, supplier due diligence, and product quality inspections.

Unlike traditional B2B marketplaces where suppliers self-register, VVI personally evaluates manufacturers before recommending them to buyers.

Our goal is simple:

**Reduce sourcing risks and build trust in international trade.**

---

# About DVS Dolor Marketing

DVS Dolor Marketing is an established appliance and electronics retailer based in Daet, Camarines Norte, Philippines.

For years, DVS has supplied homes and businesses with appliances, electronics, and other consumer products while building long-term relationships with manufacturers and suppliers.

Operating in the retail industry has given DVS firsthand experience with:

- Importing products
- Evaluating manufacturers
- Negotiating with suppliers
- Maintaining product quality
- Managing inventory
- Understanding customer expectations

This practical experience became the foundation for Victor Vanguard International.

Rather than keeping this sourcing expertise internal, the company aims to make it available to businesses worldwide through VVI.

# Why VVI Exists

Over years of importing products, the team recognized a common problem in international sourcing:

Finding suppliers is easy.

Finding suppliers you can trust is difficult.

Many online marketplaces provide thousands of supplier listings, but buyers often have limited visibility into whether a manufacturer is legitimate, capable, or consistently delivers quality products.

VVI was created to bridge this trust gap.

Instead of simply listing suppliers, VVI independently evaluates manufacturers through factory visits, business verification, product inspections, and quality assessments before introducing them to buyers.
